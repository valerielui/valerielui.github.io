about Daily Trojan & Online Projects Team

The Daily Trojan (DT) is the student-run publication at USC. At DT, the online projects team is one of the 20+ teams and sits on the digital side of the publication. We are an interdisciplinary team that creates multimedia websites and photo diaries about major themes and events on campus.

what I did

As the PM on the online projects team, I managed a team of 8 web designers and front-end developers to develop 5 websites over the course of three semesters. My main tasks on each website project included:

1

Developing project briefs
  • Establish design system & strategy for websites
  • Conduct competitor research and to clarify website strategy
  • Coordinate cross-functionally with Arts and Design, Features, and News teams to align vision across all teams
  • Creating low-fidelity mockups of the website using Figma

2

QA Testing
  • Test draft iterations and identify any bugs and areas for edits

3

Project Management
  • Present to 5+ product stakeholders and leadership regarding progress and receive feedback
  • Identify resource allocation across all teams to support asset creation and product development
