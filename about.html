about me


my design journey started in product design classes i took throughout middle and high school. it was in these classes where i fell in love with design thinking, leveraging my passion for design to create a bigger impact and support a goal my user would like to achieve.

as i’ve grown up, i’ve furthered my skills in functional design strategy, technical skills in web, ui/ux design, and front-end development, as well as developed business acumen + data analytics skills during my time at USC, forging my identity as an interdisciplinary creative today.

it's a lot - i know, but this multi-hyphenate skillset is how i plan on creating a lasting impact in our world. if that sounds interesting to you, let's connect! i'd love to grab coffee :)
